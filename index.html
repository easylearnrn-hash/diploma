Pre-Licensure Registered Nursing Program (RN)

I. Foundational Nursing Sciences

Course Title Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Medical Terminology Medical vocabulary, abbreviations, clinical language, documentation standards Safe & Effective Care Environment
Human Anatomy & Physiology Cellular biology, tissues, organ systems, homeostasis Physiological Integrity
Growth & Development Across the Lifespan Erikson, Piaget, Kohlberg, age-specific care (infant → older adult) Health Promotion & Maintenance
Nursing Fundamentals Basic nursing skills, vital signs, infection control, safety, patient-centered care Safe & Effective Care Environment

II. Pharmacological & Therapeutic Foundations

Course Title Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Pharmacology Pharmacokinetics, pharmacodynamics, medication administration, adverse effects Physiological Integrity
Medication Suffixes & Drug Classes Drug classifications, prototypes, nursing implications Physiological Integrity
Fluids, Electrolytes & Nutrition Acid–base balance, IV therapy, nutrition support Physiological Integrity

III. Adult Health & Medical-Surgical Nursing (System-Based)

System Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Cardiovascular Nursing Dysrhythmias, heart failure, hypertension, ACS, shock Physiological Integrity
Respiratory Nursing Oxygenation, ventilation, asthma, COPD, pneumonia, ARDS Physiological Integrity
Neurological Nursing Stroke, seizures, ICP, spinal cord injury Physiological Integrity
Renal & Urinary Nursing AKI, CKD, dialysis, fluid balance Physiological Integrity
Endocrine Nursing Diabetes, thyroid, adrenal disorders Physiological Integrity
Gastrointestinal & Hepatic Nursing Liver disease, bowel disorders, GI bleeding Physiological Integrity
Musculoskeletal Nursing Fractures, arthritis, mobility disorders Physiological Integrity
Integumentary / Burns & Skin Wound care, burns, pressure injuries Physiological Integrity
Sensory Systems (Eye & ENT) Vision/hearing disorders, ENT infections Physiological Integrity
Oncology Nursing Cancer pathophysiology, chemotherapy, symptom management Physiological Integrity

IV. Specialty & Population-Focused Nursing

Course Title Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Maternal & Newborn Nursing Pregnancy, labor, postpartum, neonatal care Health Promotion & Maintenance
Reproductive & Sexual Health Family planning, STIs, reproductive disorders Health Promotion & Maintenance
Pediatric Nursing Growth milestones, pediatric illnesses, family-centered care Health Promotion & Maintenance
Mental Health Nursing Therapeutic communication, psychiatric disorders, crisis intervention Psychosocial Integrity
Autoimmune & Infectious Disorders Immune dysfunction, infection control, isolation Physiological Integrity
Psycho-Social Aspects of Care Coping, stress, loss, cultural considerations Psychosocial Integrity
Gerontology Aging physiology, chronic illness, end-of-life care Health Promotion & Maintenance

Course Title Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Community & Public Health Nursing Epidemiology, disease prevention, home health, disaster nursing Health Promotion & Maintenance
Leadership & Management in Nursing Delegation, prioritization, supervision, staffing Safe & Effective Care Environment
Ethics & Legal Aspects of Nursing Scope of practice, informed consent, HIPAA, malpractice Safe & Effective Care Environment
Evidence-Based Practice & Quality Improvement Research basics, patient safety, clinical outcomes Safe & Effective Care Environment

Course Title Core Content Areas NCLEX Alignment
Clinical Judgment & Decision-Making NGN case studies, prioritization, SATA, trends analysis All NCLEX Categories
